About Us

Meet the eyes behind the lens

My name is Gurmeet Singh.

I am a wedding photographer and the heart behind Studio Vision Click.

My journey into wedding photography began with a simple yet profound realization: every love story is a unique masterpiece waiting to be told. I believe the most beautiful moments aren't always grand gestures, but often the soft glances, the shared laughter, and the unspoken emotions exchanged on your most special day. At Studio Vision Click, our passion is to freeze these fleeting instances, transforming them into a timeless visual narrative that truly reflects your authentic connection.

We are committed to delivering not just photographs, but a cherished legacy. With a blend of artistic vision, meticulous attention to detail, and a warm, personal approach, we ensure your comfort and capture every emotion with genuine care. Your memories are safe in our hands, crafted into a collection you will treasure for generations.
